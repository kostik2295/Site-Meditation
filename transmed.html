Transcendental meditation

Transcendental meditation (TM) is a special meditation technique that uses the repetition of a mantra to achieve a state of deep relaxation and peace. TM was developed in the 1950s by the Indian meditation teacher Maharishi Mahesh Yogi and has since gained wide recognition and popularity.During TM practice, you sit down in a comfortable position, close your eyes and begin to repeat the mantra quietly. A mantra is a special word or phrase that represents a sound vibration. It is important to note that the mantras used in TM are personal and are transmitted by the meditation teacher to each practitioner individually.

Advantages of transcendental meditation:

  • Stress and Anxiety Reduction: TM is known for its ability to reduce stress and anxiety levels. During TM practice, the body experiences deep physiological relaxation, which helps to reduce the production of stress hormones and creates a feeling of calm and well-being. This allows you to effectively cope with everyday stressful situations and improves your overall mental state.

  • Improving Physical and Mental Health: Regular TM practice can lead to lower blood pressure, improved heart function and immune system. It also helps to strengthen the nervous system, increase energy and improve the quality of sleep. TM gives the body the opportunity to recover and relax, which has a beneficial effect on the overall physical condition.

  • Improving Cognitive Functions: TM can improve cognitive functions such as concentration, memory and attention. Practice helps to reduce anxiety, frees the mind from negative thoughts and allows you to focus better on tasks and goals. It helps to increase productivity and efficiency in work or study.

  • Increasing creativity and intuition: TM promotes the disclosure of the potential of creative thinking and the development of intuition. Thanks to deep relaxation and the elimination of mental noise, new ideas and insights arise. You can better understand yourself and your internal processes, which helps you make more thoughtful and intuitive decisions.

  • Improving emotional well-being: TM can help to cope with emotional stress and increase the level of happiness and satisfaction. Practice helps to harmonize the emotional state and helps to develop a positive attitude towards yourself and the world around you. It allows you to better manage your emotions, facilitates the process of letting go of negative feelings and creates awareness about your emotions.

  • Ease of practice: TM is a relatively simple technique that can be easily mastered with the help of a qualified instructor. The practice can be performed at any time and in any convenient place, without requiring special equipment or space. This makes TM accessible and convenient for inclusion in everyday life.

Transcendental meditation is a deep and effective practice that can bring many positive changes to your life. It helps to create harmony between body and mind, provides inner peace and tranquility, and also develops your potential in all spheres of life.
